WHO WE ARE

Reliable Laboratory Testing Services W.L.L

We are a state-of-the-art independent testing laboratory based in The Kingdom of Bahrain, dedicated to delivering accurate, reliable, and high-quality analytical services across a broad spectrum of industries.

With a focus on Water & Wastewater, Environmental, Construction, Food, Industrial Chemicals, Petroleum Products, Solid and Liquid Waste, our facility is equipped with cutting-edge instrumentation and operated by a skilled team of chemists, technicians, and quality professionals.

We operate our laboratory in accordance with ISO/IEC 17025 quality management standards, ensuring the highest level of technical competence and data integrity.
