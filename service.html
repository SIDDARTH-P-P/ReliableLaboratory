Water & Wastewater Testing

Our water testing facility provides comprehensive analysis of potable, process, and wastewater to ensure compliance with process industries, local and international health and environmental standards.

All analyses are performed as per APHA, EPA, WHO and ISO standard methods, with a focus on safety, sustainability, and regulatory adherence.

Services Include:

  • Drinking water quality testing (Physical, Chemical & Microbiological)
  • Industrial Raw water & Treated Effluents testing
  • Wastewater treatment plant monitoring
  • Desalination and process water analysis (Boiler water, Cooling water etc.)
  • Ground water analysis

Environmental Analysis

We provide end-to-end environmental monitoring and analytical services designed to help industries, municipalities, and private entities assess their environmental impact and operational requirements.

Our laboratory specializes in the testing of air, soil, and water samples to determine contamination levels, pollutant sources, and compliance with local and international environmental regulations.

Key Tests Include:

  • Air quality monitoring (Ambient and Indoor)
  • Soil contamination analysis and heavy metal detection
  • Groundwater and surface water quality & contamination assessment
  • Gas Emissions and effluent testing for industries
  • Solid wastes, Sludge, Waste oil Analysis

Construction Materials Testing

Reliable Laboratory Testing Services W.L.L ensures that construction materials meet the Local and International standards.

We conduct precise laboratory and field testing on soil, concrete, aggregates, steel, cement, Fly ash, Micro silica and asphalt as per ASTM, BS, GSO and AASHTO standards.

Testing Capabilities:

  • Chemical analysis of Soil, Concrete, Aggregate, Cement, Fly ash, Micro Silica and Bitumen
  • Soil classification and compaction testing
  • Concrete strength and durability assessment
  • Aggregate gradation and quality testing
  • Steel reinforcement testing

Petroleum Products Testing

Reliable Laboratory Testing Services W.L.L provides physical and chemical testing for fuels, lubricants, and petroleum derivatives to assess product performance, contamination, and compliance with ASTM and API standards.

Testing Capabilities:

  • Flash point, viscosity, and density determination
  • Sulfur and carbon residue content
  • Distillation range and water content analysis
  • Lubricant degradation and oxidation testing
  • Elemental Analysis
  • Total Acid number and Base Number

Food & Agricultural Products Testing

We offer comprehensive microbiological and chemical testing to ensure food safety from production to distribution.

All tests are conducted in accordance with BAM, ISO and GSO standards, and Bahrain Ministry of Health regulations.

Testing Scope:

  • Microbial contamination (Salmonella, E. coli, Listeria, etc.)
  • Environmental Microbiology testing
  • Elemental analysis & Heavy metals
  • Nutritional Analysis (proteins, fats, Ash, carbohydrates Dry matter, etc.)
  • Organic & Inorganic fertilizers (Physical testing, Nutrients, NPK & Microbiological testing)

Industrial Chemicals Testing

We perform compositional analysis of raw and processed chemicals used in manufacturing, water treatment, and industrial applications to ensure the quality and purity.

Key Parameters:

  • Elemental composition and impurities
  • pH, conductivity, and density measurement
  • Volatile matter and moisture content
  • Trace element and additive analysis
  • Chemical purity verification
  • Contaminant identification

Solid & Liquid Waste Testing

Our waste testing services help industries identify, characterize, and manage hazardous and non-hazardous waste safely and responsibly.

Key Services:

  • Leachate and heavy metal analysis (TCLP)
  • Hazardous waste classification and toxicity testing
  • Sludge and solid residue characterization
  • Compliance audits and waste treatment monitoring
  • Disposal recommendations and documentation
