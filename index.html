OUR CORE TESTING SERVICES

Explore Our Specialized Laboratory Capabilities

Reliable Laboratory Testing Services W.L.L offers a wide range of analytical and monitoring solutions across sectors including water, environment, construction, petroleum, food, chemicals, and waste.

Water & Wastewater Testing

Comprehensive testing of drinking, process, and wastewater to ensure compliance with APHA, EPA, WHO, and ISO standards.

Environmental Analysis

Air, soil, and water quality assessments to identify contaminants and support EPA, ISO, and Bahrain EPA compliance.

Construction Materials Testing

Testing of soil, concrete, cement, aggregates, steel, and asphalt as per ASTM, BS, GSO, and AASHTO standards.

Petroleum Products Testing

Physical and chemical analysis of fuels, lubricants, and petroleum derivatives to meet ASTM and API standards.

Food & Agricultural Products Testing

Microbiological and chemical testing for food safety, nutritional analysis, and fertilizer quality control.

Industrial Chemicals Testing

Compositional and impurity analysis of industrial chemicals ensuring quality, purity, and process reliability.

Solid & Liquid Waste Testing

Detailed analysis and classification of hazardous and non-hazardous waste for safe disposal and regulatory compliance.
